WHAT WE DO

Our Vision

To provide leadership and innovation in global Shipping business and community life by delivering a flexible portfolio of services built on quality, safety, honesty, vigor and a commitment to long term business relationships.

Our Mission

ABSA is a worldwide service provider dedicated to building long-term relationships with customers, staff and suppliers. We are committed to delivering integrated services to the Shipping, Logistics, Marine and related markets at the highest levels of quality and safety. Our goal has always been to provide excellent customized services in a cost effective manner without compromising on quality of services.

About ABSA Group

(ABSA) was incorporated as a private limited company in 1985. Since that time the company thrived to become a professional entity providing integrated services to its valued clients. Al Barrak Group is now considered as a multinational pool of professionally qualified people running the various operations of the group. Our management’s approach towards the business is pragmatic, practical and an ideal blend of regional and international established values. Our goal has always been to provide excellent customized services to our customers in a cost effective manner without compromising the quality of services. Our Group abides by our Chairman's corporate motto of "FAIR PLAY" and "CLIENT SUPREMACY"
