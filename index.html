ABOUT US

ABSA Group was incorporated as a private limited company in 1985, founded by an enterprising Saudi Marine Captain, Ibrahim Barrak Al Omani, educated in United Kingdom and served as senior Ranking Officer on board reputed international shipping lines. He has also held senior Executive positions on shore, port and terminals in the Middle East. He has served as Chairman for many organizations and has more than three decades of unrivalled maritime, commercial and navigational experience. Under the professional leadership of Capt. Ibrahim Barrak Al Barrak Group has achieved phenomenal growth. Group’s goal has always been to provide excellent customized / competitive services to clients and strictly abide by group’s Chairman motto of “FAIR PLAY” and “CLIENT SUPREME”.

Shipping
ABSA Group established itself as leading Shipping Agents with offices all over the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, later expanding their offices & activities to various parts of the world.
Transportation
Group’s transportation fleet consists of Heavy Duty Mercedes and Iveco (Eurotech & Stralis Models) Tractor units complimented by a range of 30 tons flat bed trailers, container chassis and low bed trailers.
Warehouse Storage
Clients cargoes will be stored safe & monitored with digital cameras & security port teams around the clock.
Freight Forwarding
Team of dedicated professional staff, conveys ample testimony to the determined spirit for achieving quality and leadership in all pursuits.
Air Freight
Group has air cargo licenses for all major airports in the Kingdom and have forwarding arrangements with major air freight forwarders in the world.
Terminal Storage
ABSA Group started it’s first Terminal in Dammam and later expanded its facility in Jubail and Jeddah.
Logistics
We have joint operations with various international logistics providers for comprehensive door-to-door services.
Trading
Our specialized team of experts have been dealing in bulk Barley, Rice, Waste-Oil, Lubricants, Soyabeans, Scrap Alluminium , copper and Rubber.
ABSA Tours and Travels
Our Travel Office offer International/Domestic Ticketing, Hotel Booking, Tour Package and Cargo Services.
