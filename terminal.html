ABSA Jeddah Terminal & Storage Yard at KHUMRAH

Recently ABSA Group’s Terminal & Storage facility has been commissioned at Khumrah Jeddah Land, with following specifications and facilities:

Total area of land. Over 100,000 sq. meters fully ashpalted.
Area dedicated  for  Container & Break-bulk cargo  Storage / for logistics – consolidation services. Over 30,000 Sq. meters
Fully equiped work-shop facility to repair & service Truck / trailers and other small vehicles.
Electricity requirements provided by 2 Diesel operated generators of 200 KW each.
Truck-heads Presently 20 Truck heads but gruadually planning to increase number to 40 in near future.
Trailers & Container chasis 20 Container chasis + 5 flat bed trailers.
Kalmar Reach Stackers 2 Nos.
Empty Stacker 1

Yard:

2 lands of 50’000 sqm each

Weighbridge:

An 80 tones weighbridge for scaling all types of containers.

Workshop:

23 employees taking care of repairing all kinds of trailers, chassis, trucks & heavy duty equipments. Workshop equipped with state of the art repairing tools taking into consideration that “SAFETY COMES FIRST”.

Dispatch Office:

17 employees responsible of monitoring shipping line containers in terms of releasing & receiving, plus deliveries of full containers for import & empty containers for export to all destination in the kingdom.

Scope of business:

  • Receiving of empty boxes.
  • Receiving of full boxes.
  • Storage of empty & full boxes.
  • Shuttling of empty & full boxes to the port on vessels’ arrival.
  • Cleaning of boxes, dry clean &/or wash with steam & chemical products.
  • Removal of hazardous stickers of the boxes.
  • Lift on & lift off for empty & full containers.
  • Empty boxes release for export as per shipping line instruction.
  • Stuffing & de-stuffing all kind of cargo from CFS area & for export boxes.
  • General cargo handling.
  • Delivery of containers to any destination in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
  • Repairing damaged containers.
