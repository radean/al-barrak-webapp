ABSA SHIPPING AGENCIES CO. LLC

Introduction

AL BARRAK SHIPPING AGENCIES CO LLC (ABSA) was established in Dubai in 2004 as a shipping and freight forwarding company. Our core activities for Al Barrak Shipping Agencies Co LLC are Ship Agency, Freight Forwarding Sea & Air, Chartering – Sea & Air, Project and Break Bulk handling, NVOCC operations , ISO tank operation , Customs clearance, Expertise in specialized cargo handling , Cruise vessel – Medical Handling & Transportation and All types of Value added services. Al Barrak Shipping (ABSA) covers UAE with 4 offices in UAE.

Keeping the fast growing need of Logistics HUB in Dubai, ABSA Management invested into the facilities in the Jebel Ali Free Zone and established a subsidiary company in the Free Zone called as AL BARRAK LOGISTICS in Jebel Ali Free Zone which started the operation in Year 2005.

Al Barrak Logistics offer comprehensive & flexible logistic services and solutions that caters the entire supply chain management and offers our clients warehousing & distribution and other value added solutions that integrate with the inbound and outbound transportation activities.

Our total warehouse capacity is 25,000 cbm in 3 modern facilities spanning in 19,500 m2 plot size and having 18 ramps for easy flow of inward and outward cargo handling.

Our logistics & distribution center is capable to provide services to any– small, medium, large enterprises and Multi-National companies.
