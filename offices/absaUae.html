ABSA SHIPPING AGENCIES CO. LLC

Introduction

AL BARRAK Group established in UAE in 2004 with an initial set up of shipping and freight forwarding under the name of Al Barrak Cargo System.

In 2005 seeing the potential of UAE economy Al Barrak Group established its own Logistics facility in Jebel Ali Free Zone known as Al Barrak Logistics having huge warehouses with a capacity of 20,000 sq. meters with 3 warehouses wherein one warehouse is temperature controlled between 18 to 24 degree Celsius.

In 2011 Al Barrak Cargo System changed its name to Al Barrak Shipping Agencies Co. LLC with offices in Dubai Silicon Oasis, Bur Dubai, Jebel Ali Free Zone (South) of FIATA and Abu Dhabi. Member of DSAA – Dubai Shipping Agents Associations and NAFL – National Associationof Freight and Logistics.

Al Barrak Group in UAE have three office as Al Barrak Shipping Agencies Co. LLC , Al Barrak Logistics and Al Barrak General Trading Co. Al Barrak Shipping Agencies Co. LLC is actively involved in Ship Agency, Freight Forwarding Services , Projects and Break-Bulk cargo services, Marine services , Land Transportation within GCC, NVOCC operations, CFS Consol operations, Customs Clearance and all type of value added services.

Al Barrak Logistics is actively involved in Warehouse and Distribution, Consolidation, and all type of value added services.

Al Barrak General Trading concentrates on Sponsorship, Franchising, Third party distribution and Agency agreement with multinational companies and assists them in business set up and operations in UAE , GCC countries.

Ship Agency :

Al Barrak Group is one of the known group in the Shipping Agency business handling various type of ships and shipping activities having good relationship with local authorities of port and customs.

Services

Freight Forwarding Services: Sea Freight:

Our sea freight product is very strong in handling FCL and LCL On all routes with guaranteed transit times. We offer inbound and outbound consolidation services. ABSA has its own container stack in Jebel Ali and we file our own Manifest, Load List and Discharge List .

SEA – AIR shipment are handle with most care and containers discharged at top priority to meet the SEA-AIR requirements and ensure the cargo is transshipped by first available Air services to reach the final destinations.

Air Freight

We are a global air freight service provider, and has strategic alliances with prime Airlines on a regional as well as a global We offer complete solutions in air-air, sea-air, air-sea and consolidation services. We can also handle charters for special projects, oversized cargo and dangerous goods. Apart from above services freight forwarding services , we are specialized in the following services .

  • Project and Break Bulk Cargo Services
  • NVOCC Operations
  • CFS Consol Operations
  • Customs Clearance
  • Land Transportation Local & GCC Countries
  • Value Added Services

Marine Services:

We provide services such as Towage & Lighterage , Ship Chartering , Ship supplies and Ship spares , Ship owner matters, Marine survey , ISO tank handling operations etc.

Logistics and Distribution Division:

Al Barrak Logistics Division in Jebel Ali Free Zone is a fully integrated supply chain management involving, supply chain operations, re-packing, Labeling, shrink wrapping, bar coding, consolidation, de-consolidation, palatalization, de-palatalization, local transportation etc.

The Warehouse facility is equipped with 13 Dock levelers, Cargo handling equipment like Forklifts, Reach Trucks and Hand pallet movers. 24 x 7 Security with 70 CCTV cameras. Warehouse Management System – ATMS with RF Technology.
