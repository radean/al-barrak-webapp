ABSA CANADA INTERNATIONAL

Introduction

We are a Canadian – based International Freight Forwarding Company offering wide range of logistics solutions. From Air / Sea freight shipping to Warehousing we cover all aspects of logistics tailored according to customer’s requirements. Along with highly competitive rates and our global agency networks/partners combining with our own office, we offer a dedicated personalized service to our clients from planning of shipment till it reaches the desired destination.

Credentials

  • ABSA CANADA INTERNATIONAL is a part of Al-Barrak Group, KSA incorporated in 1985 and established itself as leading Shipping Agent with complete Logistics Solutions.
  • Apart from Canada Office we have our own offices India, Singapore, Iraq, Sudan, UAE and Saudi Arabia, Japan and very strong partners/associates in CHINA, South East Asia and Indian Sub-continent area.
  • We are proud member of WCA (World Cargo Alliance) Family- having competitive global coverage with almost every country.
  • We are a member of Brampton Board of Trade.
