Freight Consolidation

If you think you cargo volume is not big enough for a full container, then don’t worry, we can consolidate your cargo with other shipments going to your desired destination, thus you have the option to save extra cost. We specialize in Pakistan , India & China area for LCL Cargo. ABSA provides freight transportation services identical to that provided by a vessel owning common carrier, but without the constraints and overhead costs associated with owning and operating a fleet of vessels. For smaller, less container load (“LCL”), shipments, ABSA will consolidate your cargo with other freight. By consolidating the bookings of many large shipping volumes, ABSA offers a competitive edge over other shipping companies. Through our worldwide network of professional agents, we will deliver your cargo to its destination in a timely manner. ABSA is able to offer optimum cost effective shipping services to every destination. ABSA services embrace not only port to port, door to port, door to door, but also inland transshipment in several countries worldwide. Our personnel have extensive experience in handling, receiving and stuffing export LCL freight into steamship containers. The most important part of this service is to maximize the utility of the cubic capacity of cargo containers so that you enjoy the reduced rate that results from packing cargo more densely into the container. Packing, crating, blocking, bracing, securing, paneling, and platforming are part of an efficient freight consolidation. Our trained expert personnel are capable of performing all of these tasks with each and every container for the sole purpose of reducing the cost of your shipment.

Land Transportation

NORTH AMERICAN SURFACE TRANSPORTATION:

ABSA offer complete North American coverage for all your LTL, Partials, and FTL moves.

Canada Domestic, USA Domestic, or Canada/USA/Mexico cross-border moves are handled promptly and efficiently by our skilled and experienced operations staff.

Whatever your need, Dry Vans, Flat Decks, Floats, Lift-Gates, or Climate Controlled, ABSA’s extensive pool of C-TPAT, PIP certified carrier partners can accommodate any or all of your standard or specialized handling requirements.

ABSA Surface North America differs from conventional common carriers in that your materials do not pass through a hub and spoke system of terminals en-route to destination. The majority of our North American Surface moves are on a “Load & Go” basis, meaning that the same trailer unit which your goods were loaded on is the trailer which will deliver your goods to your client.

Less Handling = Less Risk.

ABSA Surface North America will also quote you based on Volume of trailer used versus a per cwt. charge based on class. This results in clearer, easier to understand rate quotes and invoicing, saving you time and money on your administrative processes.
