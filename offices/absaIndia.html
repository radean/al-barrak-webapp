Introduction

ABSA INDIA International Pvt. Ltd., was established in 2014. We started our operations in India with our own office in Chennai and several other states in India. We are actively engaged in all modes and methods of transportation in INDIA and can offer the full range of industry related ancillary services.

We are always looking for opportunities to expand our service portfolio and would therefore welcome any enquiries from you where you believe that we may be able to assist.

Sea Freight Division

We (ABSA India) are capable to handle shipments at Nhava Sheva, Chennai, Kandla, Mundra, Kolkatta, Coimbatore, Tuticorin. Backed up by experienced shipping documentation, equipment controllers and qualified Master Mariners to supervise & manager operations. Experienced in handling regular Container, Break-Bulk, ISO Tanks, Flexi Tanks, General Cargo, Project Cargo and RORO cargo to India at any ports.

Air Freight Division

Our Air Freight Divisions are located near all International Airport locations in India, With our Worldwide network of Officers / agents, we can provide cost effective and timely air cargo routing from/to any port of the world. We provide domicile cargo handling on DDU and DDP basis for our Local / Overseas Customers.

Land Transport

We can provide Trailers, Container Chassis and Low Bed Trailers. We undertake back loading of empty containers. With the combined practical experience of our team of specialists, we can formulate a safe, expeditious and trouble free transport management for projects, door-to-door delivery and stuffing / de-stuffing of the cargo as per specific requirements.
