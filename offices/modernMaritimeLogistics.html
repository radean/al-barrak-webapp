Introduction

Modern Maritime Logistics (MML) was incorporated in 2001 by the Al-Barrak Group, to offer innovative and effective supply chain management solutions to companies throughout Saudi Arabia with aims to be the leading supply chain management specialist in the region. From handling one aspect of your distribution to out-sourcing your entire supply chain, Modern Maritime Logistics provides tailor-made services to exactly fit your requirements. As a sales and marketing oriented company, we truly view our arrangement with our associates overseas as an integral part of our business, and we ensure everything possible is done to secure and maintain these interests. This inevitably grows to form the close working relationship we enjoy with our agents worldwide. Here at Modern Maritime Logistics, we pride ourselves on having the ability to offer our wide client base a service that is second to none. Over the years, we have made strenuous efforts to promote ourselves as a “service orientated” company. Despite making service our number one priority, we are also aware of the need to remain competitive with a rate structure that meets with our client’s requirements. Every effort is made to run the most efficient and effective service, which helps us to have our prospective client walk in our office, just coming to know of our reputation in the market.
