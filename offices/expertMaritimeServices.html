Introduction

Expert Maritime Service is engaged in Liner, Freight Forwarding and Maritime Survey activities.

Our Freight Forwarding division provides a complete range of services including Import and Export transportation solutions by Air, Sea, Rail and Road and its combination to meet customer requirements. Also involved in ocean freight consolidations worldwide through various International Freight Forwarders covering major trading regions to and from North America, Europe and the Far East and world wide.

We offer a complete and fully integrated door to door cargo-handling service from the Kingdom and can formulate a custom designed logistic package direct from manufacturer in the country of origin to consignee in Saudi Arabia. We believe in providing the most efficient service. As such, our staff has been trained to closely monitor the cargo movement at all stages an d keep you regularly informed. We have a worldwide network of corresponding agents to serve your comprehensive needs.

We are also active in project freight forwarding and undertake full-scale co-ordination both in Kingdom and Overseas. Our experienced team in the freight forwarding / clearance department can also help you in initial project mobilisation and can provide constant monitoring and liaison at your site location throughout the Kingdom.
