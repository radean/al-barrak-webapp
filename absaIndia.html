Introduction

ABSA India is a total logistics company at Chennai (Corporate office) and Mumbai. We are subsidiary company of AL BARRAK grouo, Saudi Arabia. Our other overseas branches are located UAE, Singapore, Iraq, Canada, USA & Japan.

ALBARRAK Group was incorporated as a private limited company in 1985, founded by an enterprising Saudi Marine Captain, Ibrahim Barrak Al Omani. Under the professional leadership of Capt. Ibrahim Barrak, Al Barrak Group has achieved phenomenal growth. A multinational pool of professionally qualified people runs the various operations of the group. Our management team's approach towards the business is pragmatic, practical and an ideal blend of regional and international established values. Group’s goal has always been to provide excellent customized / competitive services to clients and strictly abide by group’s Chairman motto of "FAIR PLAY" and "CLIENT SUPREME".
